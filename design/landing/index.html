Xenon

Voice dictation that never leaves your laptop.

Speak into any app and Xenon types clean, punctuated text — 100% on your device, no cloud, no account.

Download for Windows · 1.7 GB Windows 10/11 · unzip & run · no installer

Why on-device

Your voice never leaves the room.

Recording, transcription, and the grammar cleanup all run locally, on your own CPU/GPU. Nothing is uploaded, nothing is logged to a server, and it keeps working with no internet connection at all — because there's no connection to lose.

Xenon

Audio and text stay on your machine. No account, no subscription, works offline, nothing to leak.

Typical cloud dictation

Your voice is streamed to a server to be transcribed, usually tied to an account, and useless without a connection.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Hold-to-talk, anywhere

Hold F9, speak, release — works in your browser, editor, chat, or email, in any text box on Windows.

Automatic cleanup

An on-device AI adds grammar and punctuation, so what you say comes out as text you'd actually send.

Handles self-corrections

Say "send it to John, sorry, Jane" and Xenon writes "send it to Jane" — no manual cleanup.

Custom dictionary

Teach it names, jargon, and terms that a generic model would otherwise mangle.

Lightweight & private

No account, no telemetry, no background phone-home. Works fine on a plane.

Light & dark paperback themes

A quiet, editorial look that matches whichever theme your system prefers.

How it works

Three steps, no setup wizard.

  1. 1

    Download & unzip

    Grab the archive below and unzip it anywhere you like.

  2. 2

    Run whispr-app.exe

    First launch loads the on-device AI model, which takes about 30–60 seconds. After that it starts instantly.

  3. 3

    Hold F9 and speak

    Click into any text box, hold F9, talk, and release. Your words appear, cleaned up.

Heads up

A few honest notes
