# 🎙️ Try Xenon — voice dictation app

Hold a key, talk, and it types clean text into *any* app, with grammar
auto-fixed. Runs 100% on your own PC — no cloud, no account, nothing leaves
your computer.

*Windows 10/11 only · you'll need a microphone*

## 1. Download
👉 https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1R1wEGZG2CWqkxdcNG_hf3gcN0FlPdh9c?usp=sharing

Download `Xenon-windows.zip` (~1.7 GB — give it a few minutes).

## 2. Unzip
Right-click the downloaded file → **Extract All** → pick a spot (Desktop is
fine). Open the unzipped **Xenon** folder.

## 3. Run it
Double-click **`whispr-app.exe`**.

- Windows may say *"Windows protected your PC"* — that's just because it's a
  small indie app. Click **More info → Run anyway** (it's safe; nothing goes
  online).
- First launch takes ~30–60 seconds to load the AI. A settings window opens,
  and an **X** icon appears in your system tray (bottom-right near the clock —
  click the **^** arrow if you don't see it).

## 4. Dictate anywhere
- Click into any text box — browser, WhatsApp, Notepad, email, anything.
- **Hold `F9`, talk, then let go.**
- Your words get typed in with grammar cleaned up (takes a few seconds — the
  AI runs on your own CPU).
- Correcting yourself works too: say *"send it to John, sorry, I mean Jane"*
  → it types *"send it to Jane."*

## 5. Customize (optional)
Right-click the tray **X** icon → **Settings** to change the hotkey, switch
light/dark, or add words it keeps mishearing (like names).
